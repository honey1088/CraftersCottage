An authentic platform for pure craft forms.

CraftersCottage is a digital marketplace for some of the world's oldest and best craft forms. To promote different crafts there is a need for a centralized hub that fosters a community of artisans, and provides tools for effective marketing and collaboration. In the absence of such a platform, artisans often struggle with the complexities of online commerce, hindering the growth of their businesses and limiting the accessibility of authentic, handmade products to a global audience.

Our Mission

Embrace the timeless masterpieces created by India's Master Artisans

We seek to offer a fair-trade platform for handicraft products. Our primary aim is to support the handicraft workers from different parts of the country. Each design offered by us is a legacy for generations. We try to keep the different forms of art alive by by giving these products recognition on online marketplace.

Celebrating world's oldest craft forms

Dhokra art(or Dokra art) is among one of the oldest traditional techniques of art form.The art is a non-ferrous metal casting technique known as lost-wax casting.In India, it is practiced for over 4,000 years, said to date back to the era of the Indus Valley Civilisation.The lost wax technique or cire-perdue has been the medium of metal workers in Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Orissa and West Bengal. Entirely handcrafted, the unique 13-stage process of Dhokra /Bell-metal making today, is an outcome of the original craft instinct handed down through the centuries. Dhokra or Bell metal is an alloy of brass, and other metals that impart an antique effect to the castings.

Supporting eco-friendly choices

Creating awareness for craft is inextricably linked with promoting ecological choices with a few exceptions. The Indian crafts practitioner is perhaps the greatest conservationist who strives to live in harmony with his environment. While, we do our best to promote eco-friendly choices to the best extent possible, there are a few exceptions where helping livelihoods may take precedence, simply due to our commitment to help every craft form and every type of artisan from our hinterlands.

Promoting Fair trade.

Ensuring fair prices that are mutually agreed with our artisans, while providing support with ideas and help to direct their immense talent in staying contemporary and relevant to a changing world. Our cost and price models are worked out equitably between us and our artisans. Our partners and associates work tirelessly to assist artisans in increasing their skills and improving their practices through assistance with production methods, working conditions, business practices and product development. We endeavour to provide all our partners, artisans and craftsmen access to a larger marketplace and a fairer price for their products, not just by promoting what they make, but also by helping them with ideas and designs to innovate and create relevant craft products that appeal to changing tastes and modern preferences, to help them find wider markets.
